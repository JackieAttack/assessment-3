My Neighborhood

My neighborhood is a quiet little suburb located in Clearfield Utah. I've only lived here for a few months, but compared to the rundown cockroach infested appartment I where I was living previously (in downtown Salt Lake City) it's not bad.

It's kind of a developing area, but you can get everything you need here and it's not too expensive.

Restaurants

The restaurants in Clearfield aren't as good as the one's in SLC, but there are a few good finds.

  • Haru Ramen: Great ramen and japanese food.
  • Taste of India: A good indian option.
  • Chris' Cafe: I like their eggs benedict. Kinda busy in the morning.
